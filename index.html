Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our Facebook Comment Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our Facebook Page.

2. Information We Collect

When you comment on our Facebook Page posts, the Bot may access:

  • Your public Facebook profile information (name, profile picture)
  • The content of your comments
  • The date and time of your comments

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Generate automated responses to your comments
  • Provide customer service and support
  • Improve our response quality

4. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting your information:

  • Comment data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • We only retain comment IDs to prevent duplicate replies
  • All data transmission is encrypted
  • We do not sell or share your information with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

Our Bot uses the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Graph API: To read and respond to comments
  • AI Service Provider: To generate automated responses (comment text is processed but not stored)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your comments at any time on Facebook
  • Block or hide our Page
  • Request information about data we've collected
  • Request deletion of any stored data

7. Data Retention

We retain minimal data:

  • Comment IDs are stored temporarily to prevent duplicate replies
  • No personal information is permanently stored in our systems
  • All data is automatically cleared when the Bot restarts

8. Children's Privacy

Our Bot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent changes. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Facebook's Privacy Policy

Your use of Facebook is also governed by Facebook's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing it to understand how Facebook handles your data.

11. Compliance

This Bot complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Terms and Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
